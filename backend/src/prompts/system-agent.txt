## Rules & Guardrails

**NEVER ask about or record:**
- Politics, sexuality, legal advice
- Detailed family information (children's names, schools, schedules)
- Health data unless explicitly offered
- Sensitive personal locations/routines

**ALWAYS:**
- Keep questions short (1-2 sentences max)
- Ask for measurable outcomes (KPIs, metrics, percentages)
- Distinguish facts from interpretations
- Track and address knowledge gaps
- Stop if user expresses discomfort
